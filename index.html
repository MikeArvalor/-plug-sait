Accounting Services

We provide bookkeeping, accounting, payroll services and tax advice to individuals and small and medium size businesses

  • Bookkeeping

    We will keep your books in order. We use different bookkeeping software from Xero, Sage, Quickbooks to excel spreadsheets. We will also be happy to use any other software of your choice. If you need advice on what bookkeeping software is most suitable for your type of business, please ask us.

  • Self-Assessment

    We prepare tax returns for individuals with various sources of income (employment, self-employment, rental income, investment income, dividends, pension, foreign income, etc.)

  • Payroll

    We offer timely preparation of payslips and RTI submissions. We will advice on the choice of a pension provider for your employees, and we will take care of timely submission of pension contributions and pension declaration.

  • Tax Investigations

    We will deal with HMRC on your behalf if you get a tax enquiry about your Self-Assessment, Corporation Tax Return, VAT Return, PAYE or CIS Compliance.

  • VAT

    We will advise on the right VAT Scheme for your business (Standard Scheme, Flat Rate Scheme, Annual Accounting Scheme). We will make sure that your business is compliant with MTD regulations. Please ask us if you have questions about cross-border VAT.

  • Company Accounts

    Whether it is Dormant Accounts or Accounts for a Small or Medium size business, we are here to prepare them timely and professionally following by filing with Companies House and HM Revenue & Customs.

  • CIS

    Registration for CIS, verification of sub-contractors, timely submission of CIS Returns, reclaiming a CIS Refund for sub-contractors and contractors.

  • Compliance with Companies House

    We will keep your books in order. We will prepare and file Confirmation Statements and Financial Statements with Companies House on your behalf when they become due.
